Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita Pizza is another personal favorite of mine!

It is truly an Italian classic, simple yet captivating (most of the time!). Thus this recipe is perfect for beginners since it doesn't require a lot of ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs (453g) classic pizza dough ball (you can use store-bought pizza dough too)
  • ½ cup Pizza Sauce (use your preferred brand)
  • 2 cups Fresh Sliced Mozzarella
  • ¼ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 475F. Prepare all ingredients for the margherita pizza.

  2. Step 2

    3. On a floured surface, roll the dough into the desired shape. Top it off with pizza sauce, pieces of fresh mozzarella and sprinkle a bit of parmesan cheese.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake pizza for about 14 minutes or until the crust is lightly browned and the cheese is golden.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove pizza from the oven, top it off with fresh basil. Serve pizza right away, while the cheese is all melted.
